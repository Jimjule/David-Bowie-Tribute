    <h3 align="center">The man who touched the lives of millions</h3>

    <h3 align="center">Some facts about Bowie:</h5>

    <li>Born David Robert Bowie in Brixton, London on 8th January 1947.</li>

    <li>One of his pupils is permenantly dilated after he was punched during a fight at school, over a girl.</li>

    <li>George Underwood - the same person who punched him at school - remained a good friend of Bowie, even doing artwork for some of his earlier albums.</li>

    <li>His first-ever release was Liza Jane/Louie Louie Go Home in June 1964, under the name of Davie Jones with The King-Bees.</li>

    <li>He released his debut album, the self-titled David Bowie, in 1967 after playing in a host of pub and club bands.</li>

    <li>Bowie's first hit in the UK - 1969's Space Oddity - was used by the BBC in its coverage of the moon landing.</li>

    <li>Bowie's first US number one was his single Fame in 1975. It was co-written by John Lennon and features the former Beatle on backing vocals.</li>

    <li>Model Twiggy features with him on the cover for his 1973 album Pin Ups.</li>

    <li>It is estimated that Bowie sold around 140 million albums over his career.</li>

    <li>In the 1986 film Labyrinth, Bowie plays Jareth The Goblin King.</li>

    <li>Bowie appeared as Pontius Pilate in Martin Scorsese's The Last Temptation of Christ.</li>

    <li>Bowie married his wife Iman in 1992, and their daughter Alexandria was born in 2000.</li>

    <li>David Bowie died from liver cancer on 10th January 2016, just after his 69th birthday.</li>

    <h3 align="center"><i>"Without David Bowie, popular music as we know it pretty much wouldn't exist."</i>  -Moby</h3>
